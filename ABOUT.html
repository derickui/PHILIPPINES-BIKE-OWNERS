ABOUT US

Bike Register PH is a national bike registration initiative that aims to protect cyclists and promote bike safety. By registering your bike, you help create a safer and more connected cycling community.

Our mission is to prevent bike theft, aid in the recovery of lost bikes, and encourage responsible ownership among cyclists across the Philippines.
